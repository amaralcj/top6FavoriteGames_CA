Arma Reforger is a tactical military shooter set during the 1989 Cold War on the island of Everon, where players fight as U.S. or Soviet forces. It emphasizes realistic combat, teamwork, and strategy, allowing players to use authentic weapons and vehicles, and it serves as a versatile platform for the development of the new Arma 4 engine.
